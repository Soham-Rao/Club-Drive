How to Play

Single Player Rules

  • Underpopulation: A live cell with fewer than 2 neighbors dies.
  • Survival: A live cell with 2 or 3 neighbors lives on.
  • Overpopulation: A live cell with more than 3 neighbors dies.
  • Reproduction: A dead cell with exactly 3 neighbors becomes a live cell.

Multiplayer Rules

  • Immigration: New cells inherit the color of the majority of their 3 parents.
  • Winning: The game stops at Generation 1000. The player with the most live cells wins!
  • Draw Mode: Click to toggle cells. Drag to paint multiple cells.
